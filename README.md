# 🌸 Petals

A calm, minimal todo app built around gentle momentum.

**Live → [peaceful-momentum.vercel.app](https://peaceful-momentum.vercel.app)**

---

## What it is

Petals is a mindful task manager that grows with you.

Instead of rigid deadlines and stress, it offers a quiet space to hold
your intentions for the day. A cherry blossom tree blooms as you make
progress — and resets each morning, encouraging you to show up again.

---

## Features

- **Priority flow** — Tasks arrange from urgent to light, naturally
- **Living tree** — Blooms as you complete tasks each day
- **Daily reset** — Fresh start every morning. Your streak stays. The pressure doesn't.
- **Task lifespan** — Give tasks a life of 1 day, 3 days, a week, or none
- **Forgotten drawer** — Let go tasks rest here quietly, never hard deleted
- **Energy tags** — Label tasks by energy type, not just priority
- **Momentum streak** — A gentle count of days you showed up
- **Dark mode** — Soft on the eyes at any hour

---

## How to use it

**Adding a task**
Type what's on your mind. Choose a priority, an energy tag if you like,
and how long the task should live.

**Priority levels**
- 🔴 High — things that matter most today, shown first
- 🟡 Medium — steady, balanced work
- 🔵 Low — lighter things, shown softly at the bottom

**Completing a task**
Tap the circle. A petal falls. The tree grows a little.

**The tree**
Starts bare each morning. Complete tasks and watch it bloom.
Come back tomorrow and it starts again — your streak remembers you showed up.

**The forgotten drawer**
Tasks that expire or get archived live here quietly.
Restore them or let them go permanently.

**Daily reset ritual**
Each morning the app gently asks what to do with unfinished tasks —
carry forward, delay a day, or release.
